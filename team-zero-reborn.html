Team Zero Reborn

Bobzonkle July 17, 2018

Some people stopt playing, some went to different guilds. Team Zero was dead. Now it's almost time to start battling the filthy alliance once more. Team Zero will come back the next expansion. Bigger, better, Team Zero Reborn.
